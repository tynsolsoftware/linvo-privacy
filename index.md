# Privacy Policy for Linvo

**Last updated: [DATE]**

## 1. Overview

Linvo ("we", "our", or "the app") is a loyalty card, gift card, and shopping list manager for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is handled.

We are based in New Zealand and comply with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2a. Free Tier — Data We Never See

If you use Linvo without a Pro account, your data (loyalty cards, gift cards, shopping lists, and wish lists) is stored exclusively in your own Google Drive, in a private app folder that is not visible to you or to us.

**We have no access to this data.** It never passes through our servers.

Google Drive's own privacy policy governs how that data is stored. You can review it at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 2b. Crash & Diagnostic Data (All Users)

We use **Firebase Crashlytics** (Google) to collect crash reports and diagnostic information when the app encounters an error. This data includes:

- Device model and Android version
- App version
- Stack traces and error logs
- A randomly generated installation ID (not linked to your identity)

This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs. It does not include your personal cards, lists, or any content you store in the app.

**You can opt out of crash reporting at any time in the app's Settings.** If you opt out, no diagnostic data will be collected or transmitted from your device. We encourage you to leave it enabled as it helps us improve the app, but the choice is entirely yours.

### 2c. Pro Tier — Account & Sync Data *(Planned Feature)*

When Pro features (shared lists, wish list sale alerts) become available, we will collect:

- Your Google account email address, used to identify your account
- Shared list contents, stored on our servers to enable collaboration

This section will be updated with full details before Pro features are launched.

---

## 3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

- Your name, address, or payment details (payments are handled by Google Play)
- Your location
- Any information about the merchants, products, or purchases on your loyalty or gift cards
- Any advertising identifiers or behavioural tracking data

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion

**Free tier data** lives entirely in your Google Drive. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app (which removes the app data folder) or via Google Drive settings.

**Crash data** is retained by Firebase Crashlytics according to their standard retention policies (approximately 90 days).

**Pro account data** *(when available)* — you will be able to delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Linvo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.
